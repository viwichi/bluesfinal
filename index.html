About Me

Pina Colada Blues

Is an upcoming indie electronic producer from Kerala. Also known as Pina C or PCB, Kevin Shaji is Pina Colada Blues. The name Pina Colada Blues is a representation of the cocktail like genres used in his music. The music mainly consists of Indian instruments, dream/electro - pop/ trap/hybrid trap/hip-hop/rnb/carnatic and eastern infused western styles. Thenale is the debut single of Pina Colada Blues released in 2020. Pina Colada Blues has been featured on Rolling Stones India, BBC Asia Network, VH1, The New Indian Express, High on Score and other magazines, blogs, Radio networks and TV channels.

His Debut Regional Indie Album - Once Upon A Blue Moon is Out Now.
