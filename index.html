About Me

Kevin Shaji

Aka Pina Colada Blues is an upcoming indie electronic producer from Kerala. Also known as Pina C or PCB. The name Pina Colada Blues is a representation of the cocktail like genres used in his music. The music mainly consists of Indian instruments, dream/electro - pop/ trap/hybrid trap/hip-hop/rnb/carnatic and eastern infused western styles. Thenale is the debut single of Pina Colada Blues released in 2020.

Pina Colada Blues has been featured on Rolling Stones India, BBC Asia Network, VH1, The New Indian Express, High on Score and other magazines, blogs, Radio networks and TV channels.
His Debut Regional Indie Album - Once Upon A Blue Moon is Out Now.

Debut Album

Once upon a Blue Moon

It is Pina Colada Blue's Debut album and consists of 8 tracks- 7 Malayalam songs and 1 Tamil song. This is a chill indie electronic pop album fused with a lot of genres including lofi, pop, trap, indian fusion etc. The album portrays a story of a relationship from its inception to its end and moving on.
With this album, the artist has managed to bring out a fresh range of sounds to the malayalam indie scene. Try it on all major platforms.
